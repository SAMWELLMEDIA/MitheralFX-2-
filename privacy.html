Regulations

U.S. Regulatory Compliance

MitheralFX is committed to operating in full compliance with applicable United States regulations governing digital commerce, trading platforms, and online business practices.

SEC Compliance

MitheralFX adheres to guidelines provided by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) to ensure lawful operations in trading-related activities. We do not offer or facilitate unregistered securities and ensure users understand their financial risks clearly.

Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

As part of our compliance with FinCEN and U.S. AML regulations, MitheralFX maintains strict policies to detect and prevent illicit financial activities. Identity verification and transaction monitoring are core parts of our platform integrity.

Consumer Protection

MitheralFX aligns with Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines on online transparency, advertising accuracy, and consumer data privacy. Our goal is to maintain a trustworthy ecosystem for all users.

Data Protection

User data is secured using end-to-end encryption and stored in compliance with U.S. data protection standards. We never sell user information and limit access to sensitive data.

Transparency and Trust

MitheralFX upholds a code of conduct based on honesty, transparency, and user-first operations. We regularly review and update our policies in line with changes to U.S. law and industry standards.
