Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple and only takes a few minutes. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website, fill in your details, verify your email address, and you're ready to start trading. We'll guide you through each step of the process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and various cryptocurrencies. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to protect your financial information.

How can I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Make sure to create a strong, unique password that you haven't used elsewhere.

Is my money safe with MitheralFX?

Absolutely. We use state-of-the-art security measures including cold storage for funds, two-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Your funds are protected by our insurance policy and we comply with all regulatory requirements.

What are your trading fees?

Our trading fees are among the lowest in the industry. We charge a small percentage of each trade, with VIP members enjoying even lower rates. There are no hidden fees or commissions. You can view our complete fee structure in your account dashboard.
